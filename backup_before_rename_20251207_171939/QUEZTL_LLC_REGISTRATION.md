## Quick Registration Steps

### Option 1: Online Formation (Fastest - 1-2 days)

**Recommended Services**:
1. **LegalZoom** - https://www.legalzoom.com
   - Cost: ~$79 + state fees
   - Includes EIN, Operating Agreement
   - Time: 1-2 business days

2. **Incfile** - https://www.incfile.com
   - Cost: $0 + state fees (basic package)
   - Free registered agent for 1 year
   - Time: 1-2 business days

3. **Northwest Registered Agent** - https://www.northwestregisteredagent.com
   - Cost: ~$39 + state fees
   - Excellent customer service
   - Time: 1-2 business days

### Option 2: DIY State Filing (Cheapest)

**For California** (if you're in CA):
1. Go to: https://bizfilesonline.sos.ca.gov/
2. File "Articles of Organization" (Form LLC-1)
3. Cost: $70 filing fee
4. Time: 5-15 business days

**For Delaware** (recommended for tech startups):
1. Go to: https://corp.delaware.gov/
2. File "Certificate of Formation"
3. Cost: $90 filing fee
4. Time: Same day if expedited ($100 extra)

### Option 3: Stripe Atlas (Tech Startup Optimized)

**Stripe Atlas** - https://stripe.com/atlas
- Cost: $500 (includes everything)
- Automatically files in Delaware
- Includes: EIN, bank account setup, stock certificates, legal templates
- Best for: Raising venture capital
- Time: 1-2 weeks
